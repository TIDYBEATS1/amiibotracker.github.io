About AmiiboTracker

AmiiboTracker is a powerful app designed to help you keep track of all your Amiibo figures, cards, and compatible games in one place. Whether you're a casual collector or a dedicated fan, AmiiboTracker helps you organize, compare, and discover new ways to enjoy your collection.

Features

  • Track owned Amiibo by series, game compatibility, and platform.
  • Compare your collection with friends to see shared and missing Amiibos.
  • Detailed Amiibo information including usage in compatible games.
  • Dark mode and customizable themes for easy viewing.
  • Offline support to view your collection anytime, anywhere.
  • Secure sign-in with Firebase Authentication including GitHub login.
