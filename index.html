About AmiiboTracker

AmiiboTracker is your personal Amiibo collection and compatibility tracker. Whether you're a casual collector or a dedicated fan, AmiiboTracker helps you organize, compare, and discover new ways to enjoy your collection.

  • Track owned Amiibo by series, game compatibility, and platform.
  • Compare your collection with friends to see shared and missing Amiibos.
  • View detailed Amiibo information including game usage.
  • Customize your view with dark mode and themes.
  • Offline support to access your collection anytime.
  • Secure sign-in using Firebase Authentication.
  • macOS and iPadOS support coming soon.

Need help or have questions? Contact us at support@amiibotracker.co.uk.
